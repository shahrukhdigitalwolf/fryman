About Fryman

A new age Bengali and first of its kind QSR, serving the classics of Bengal alongwith popular dishes from around the globe.
The British left a strong influence in the kitchens of Kolkata, the erstwhile capital of British India. The cuisines of the continent relished by the Britishers, with the passage of time, were fine-tuned to the Indian palate and became popular delicacies of every restaurant from Park Street to "Para" streets. Kolkata then became the melting pot for multiple European cultures, like English, Portuguese, French- leading the 'City of Joy' to develop a strong taste for continental cuisine.
These delicious savouries are from MWD Food Products Pvt. Ltd. We continually strive to maintain the highest international standards of health, hygiene and food quality.
Our policy is fresh food and zero preservatives.
Our vision is to establish ourselves as the first QSR to popularize the unique Kolkata colonial fries from our kitchen to the world at large and to attain the rightful place among the popular western QSRs and mark its own niche.
